Nội dung phim (Synopsis)

Twelve years after the Second Neo Zeon War, the peace of the Earth Sphere is disrupted by "Mafty", an anti-Earth Federation government organization.

Hathaway Noa, still haunted by his past actions, joins the terrorist organization Mafty and takes on the name Mafty Navue Erin. The organization is fighting a terrorist campaign against the Earth Federation government, and they eventually manage to acquire the prototype Xi Gundam, of which Hathaway becomes the pilot. Their goal: to assassinate high-ranking Federation officials to break the corrupt grip on humanity.
